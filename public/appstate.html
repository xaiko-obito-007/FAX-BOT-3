Quick Tools

How to Get Appstate

1

Install Browser Extension

Get a cookie management extension like "EditThisCookie" or "Cookie-Editor" from your browser's extension store

2

Login to Facebook

Open Facebook in your browser and login with your account credentials

3

Export Cookies

Click the extension icon and export all cookies in JSON format

4

Copy JSON Array

Copy the entire JSON array and paste it into the textarea above

5

Save & Restart

Click "Save Appstate" button to update your bot. The system will restart automatically
